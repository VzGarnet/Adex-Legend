Fine
Combat Medic
Yaja Che, aka Fine, isn’t someone you would expect to find in the Adex Games. The child of wealthy war profiteers, she left home when she learned of the damage her family had caused and enlisted in the Frontier Corps, a humanitarian organization that aids Frontier communities in need. She’s since devoted her life to helping others and joined the Adex Games to fund the Frontier Corps with her winnings.
  • Real Name : Yaja Che
  • Age : 24
  • Home World : Psamathe
  • Tactical Ability : D.O.C. Heal Drone
  • Passive Ability : Combat Medic
  • Ultimate Ability : Care Package
