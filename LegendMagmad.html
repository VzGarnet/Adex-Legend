Magmad
Rebel Warlord
Margret Kōhere - the woman who would one day be known as Magmad - grew up in one of the dustiest, dullest corners of Salvo without any family to speak of. She was fortunate, then, to meet a kindred chaotic spirit: an adventurous young boy named Walter Fitzroy. The two were inseparable, and in their teenage years, they became mercenaries. Eventually this duo grew into the Cracked Talon - a powerful mercenary operation. It was here she earned her nom de guerre: Magmad.
  • Real Name : Margret Kōhere
  • Age : 55
  • Home World : Salvo
  • Tactical Ability : Riot Drill
  • Passive Ability : Warlord’s Ire
  • Ultimate Ability : Wrecking Ball
